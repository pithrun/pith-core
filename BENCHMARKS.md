## What CogGov-Bench Measures

CogGov-Bench is an internal governance benchmark. It evaluates whether Pith's cognitive governance behavior changes system outcomes, including:

- Stale knowledge resistance
- Context integrity
- Correction learning
- Cross-session coherence
- Recovery behavior
- Constraint adherence
- Adversarial robustness probes
